// 01 — About Me

The analyst who builds & deploys

I am Eze Festus Chukwuebuka — a Data Analyst, Data Scientist, and Machine Learning Engineer based in Enugu, Nigeria, fully available for the UK time zone. I sit at the crossroads of business intelligence and predictive modelling.

At ApTech Computer Education, I design interactive Power BI dashboards, automate monthly reporting pipelines, and build star-schema data models that improve speed and decision quality. Simultaneously, I build and deploy end-to-end ML applications — from a house rent prediction model for Argentina to a stock market anomaly detector and a movie recommendation engine, all live on Streamlit.

My undergraduate background in Applied Biochemistry from ESUT gave me rigorous scientific thinking — a discipline I now apply to every data problem. I believe a great data professional is not just a coder, but a storyteller who speaks fluently to both executives and algorithms.
